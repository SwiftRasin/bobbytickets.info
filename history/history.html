series 4a (September 1-4, 2024)
Series 4a was a milestone for bobby tickets.
It introduced bobby ticket packs, which is a system we still use today.
4 tickets in a pack, without repeat tickets in a single pack.
It also had a whopping 12 unique variants to collect, as opposed to the 8, or even 4 variants from before.
September 1-4, 2024
Series 4b came out a few weeks later.
